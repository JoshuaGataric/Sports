My Passion for Sports

Overview

From the day I was born, sports have been a central part of my life. Being born in Ann Arbor, Michigan, I became a lifelong Michigan football fan. While Saturdays are all about college football, Sundays are dedicated to supporting the Pittsburgh Steelers. Beyond football, my parents' Pittsburgh roots instilled in me a passion for the Pirates and Penguins as well.

Favorite Teams

  • Michigan Wolverines - College Football
  • Pittsburgh Steelers - NFL Football
  • Pittsburgh Pirates - MLB Baseball
  • Pittsburgh Penguins - NHL Hockey
